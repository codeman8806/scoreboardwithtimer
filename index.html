Scoreboard With Timer

Turn your phone or tablet into a clean, customizable scoreboard and game clock. Perfect for basketball, football, soccer, volleyball, hockey, table tennis, and more.

Why people use Scoreboard With Timer

  • Tap to quickly add or subtract points, goals, or sets.
  • Built-in game timer for periods, halves, and quarters.
  • Save and reopen previous games with history.
  • Works great for tournaments, school sports, rec leagues, and backyard games.

Pro features

  • Live score sharing – send a link so others can follow in real time.
  • Custom team colors and buzzer sounds.
  • Ad-free mode so you can focus on the game.
